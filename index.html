What Is Community Solar?

Your neighbor's solar farm is already saving them money.

Community solar is a shared solar farm — built on a field or large rooftop somewhere in your area — that generates electricity for an entire neighborhood. Instead of putting panels on your own roof, you subscribe to a share of that farm's output.

The farm sends power to the grid. Your utility applies a credit to your bill every month. You pay less. That's it.

  • ✓Works for renters and homeowners alike
  • ✓No equipment, no installation, no inspection required
  • ✓Same utility company, same electricity — just a lower bill
